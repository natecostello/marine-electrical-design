- Fewer engine or generator run hours

  - 👉️ less maintenance
  - 👉️ more peace and quiet
  - 👉️ less fuel consumption

- Higher engine load

  - 👉️ less glazing, fouling, maintenance,
  - 👉️ greater longevity
  - 👉️ greater watt-hours per fuel consumed
